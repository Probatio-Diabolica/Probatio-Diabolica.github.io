About Me

I’m Ayush Kaintura, and I like my software the way I like my shell configs — fast, minimal, and predictable. I’ve probably spent more time in /proc than most spend on Stack Overflow, and I’m not proud of it... but I kinda am. Whether it’s building a task queue from scratch or tinkering deep into system internals, I care about clean design, low overhead, and tools that just work — no manual needed.

Some things I enjoy:

  • Contributing to open source projects
  • Building systems that shouldn't exist, but work anyway
  • Keeping my Arch install unnecessarily optimized
  • Chasing performance and elegance in equal parts
  • Debugging things nobody asked me to touch
